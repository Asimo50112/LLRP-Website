Welcome to Lakeland Roleplay!

Lakeland Roleplay is an immersive Emergency Response Liberty County (ERLC) community based in Florida, offering realistic and high-quality roleplay experiences.

Why Join Us?

  • High-Quality Roleplay: We follow strict, realistic RP rules to ensure an immersive experience.
  • Custom Jobs & Departments: Join elite units such as SWAT, Polk County Sheriff, AMR, and many more!
  • Active Staff & Community: Our team provides 24/7 moderation to ensure a fair and enjoyable environment for all players.
  • Realistic Police Pursuits: Engage in tactical chases and strategic arrests for an action-packed RP experience.

Department of Justice (DOJ)

Our DOJ system is designed to bring real-world legal structure to our roleplay. Based on the Florida judicial system, our courts handle criminal and civil cases, ensuring fair trials and lawful enforcement. Players can become attorneys, judges, or even prosecutors, creating a dynamic and engaging legal roleplay experience.

How to Join

Joining is easy! Click here to join our Discord server, where you can start roleplaying, apply to become staff, join law enforcement, or take on any role you choose!
